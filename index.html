KEY TRAINING TERMS

  • RPE: Rate of Perceived Exertion. RPE 7 = 3 reps left. RPE 10 = max
  • CNS: Your nervous system for pop/explosiveness
  • Tempo 3-1-2: 3 seconds down, 1 second pause, 2 seconds up
  • Volume: Sets × Reps × Weight | Intensity: % of max

AUTOREGULATE & RED FLAGS

  • Sleep < 8.5 hr: Drop intensity 15%, add 20-30 min nap
  • Weight swing > 5 lb in 24 hr: Sip 32-48 oz with electrolytes, add carbs next 2 meals
  • New pain: Remove trigger, use pain-free mid-range iso 30-45s
  • Stress 5: Full recovery day, breathwork twice, easy walk + mobility only
  • Sleep < 8.5 hr or stress 4-5: Skip tempos that week
